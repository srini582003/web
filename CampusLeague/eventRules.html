Event Rules

All attendees must register for the event

Each student participant is required to carry their individual college ID card

Electronic devices such as phones, tablets, and laptops are not allowed inside the event hall.

Any form of harassment, discrimination, or inappropriate behavior towards anyone at the event is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate removal from the symposium.

Attendees are expected to maintain discipline in the event hall and its vicinity.

Any discrepancies should be reported to the faculty members immediately
